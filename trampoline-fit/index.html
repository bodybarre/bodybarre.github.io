Trampoline Hiit

HIIT, or high-intensity interval training, is a training technique in which you give all-out, one hundred percent effort through quick, intense bursts of exercise, followed by short, active, recovery periods. This type of training gets and keeps your heart rate up and burns more fat in less time.

BENEFITS

  • Aids in heart health
  • HIIT challenges you both mentally and physically
  • It’s a great way to burn fat while preserving hard-earned muscle mass
  • HIIT is a fantastic way to naturally increase your metabolism
  • HIIT allows your body to burn calories well after your session is over

Go get it. Keep it lean.
