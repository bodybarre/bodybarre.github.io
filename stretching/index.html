Splits Stretch

Let us unlock the stretching secrets.

Stiff as a board? Supple as a slinky cat? You can be either to join this class! You are never to old to pop yourself into the splits pose. For some it comes naturally and for others it takes time - but it does arrive - eventually - with hard work and commitment to the course, splits will be yours! And if you’ve already got splits - maintain them - or start to work over splits - we will always keep you challenged to work the best version of you.

Options to cater to each individual, we can make things more challenging and easier for you. Let's smash some bendy goals! Make sure you wear some comfy warm clothes for this class, and make sure you can move easily in your chosen outfit! 

Got splits? Get splits... the Can-Can just might become your favourite dance.

Stretch and Massage

A restful and rejuvenating session to keep your body a finely tuned, well oiled BOD! A soothing foam roller class to keep the muscles healthy. Roll away muscle tension and undo any knots. Learn to relax as you roll, and gain a deep tissue massage. Leave class feeling super relaxed, with a finely tuned body which now moves more freely.

A once a week must..!

Wear warm cosy clothing that you can move freely in and keep your limbs snuggled up! 

Restore your roar!

Back 2 Back

This class will help you maintain a healthy functional back whilst also increasing flexibility back bending skills. Keep your spine as strong, flexible and healthy as possible. We have created a new strong and bendy class to carefully condition and to simply stretch the back. 

Class begins with conditioning exercises to build strength in your back, hips, shoulders and wrists. The focus will then move to flexibility in the hamstrings, hips, lower and upper back and shoulders. 

Life is easier when your back supports you
