Kettlebells

Get sweating and release the ‘happy’ chemicals, get a fitter ticker, tone up, burn fat, and enjoy a healthier you.

Kettlebell training is a great way for you to reduce body fat while learning some many basic Kettlebell exercises. Whether you are a total novice or a regular kettlebell user, this class suits all abilities. Kettlebell training uses a whole body approach, targeting each muscle group for a balanced workout.

Mixing resistance training with cardiovascular training helps to sculpt the body very quickly especially when coupled with a healthy balanced diet.

We use light weight Kettlebells, therefore this workout will not bulk up the body, the system is designed to deliver cardio fat burning fitness which keeps the body lean and sculpted.
