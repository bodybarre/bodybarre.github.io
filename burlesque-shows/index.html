Burlesque Shows

Allow us to take away the "what shall we do" and "where shall we go" palaver. Spend a Saturday with the Bombshells. Enjoy a quirky Burlesque experience for an alternative treat, with unusual and stunning performances that ooze lavish visuals, extravagant fantasy, and breathtaking giggly moments!

Join us on an über glam adventure and make some wonderful memories with us. Does it tickle your fancy? Book a table today.

The important stuff

The performance venue is on the 2nd floor with two flights of stairs. We are so sorry that our venue does not have a lift or ramp. Please let us know if you need any help with the stairs.

During showtimes, we hope that we will absolutely mesmerise you, but we just want to remind you to be respectful to the performers and other audience members and to keep talking during the show to an absolute minimum.

Once payment has been received for your table your booking is final and unfortunately non-refundable, nor exchangeable, or transferable. Ensure you take note of your booking date because if you miss it, you will sadly miss out.

We are super excited to see you soon. Bring an appropriate cheer and whoop as we invite you to enjoy the spirit of varitease...

What to wear?

Get your glad rags on, a sexy little number, a super loud shirt, whatever you feel fabulous in, because we really don’t mind what you wear, no rules!

Cream Tease

Bakes & Burlesque

Spend an hour in the afternoon with the Bombshells. A unique experience for lovers of the sweet stuff and arts alike. Let us tempt you to a yummy selection of bakes and cakes, washed down with a range of hot and cold soft drinks. Vegan, gluten free, and milk alternatives available. All served up with some knockout entertainment.

Let’s set the mood for you. Upon arrival you will be shown to your table. Take the weight off your feet, have a lovely chin wag, and soak up the charming atmosphere. On the table you will find a drinks order slip for you to fill out. Once you have selected your thirst quenchers, wave the slip in the air, like you just don’t care, and your server will make your drink dreams come true! Stand by for the sugary stuff, because thats coming, to party in your mouth! After 20 minutes, the Bombshells will take centre stage as they rev up the 20 minute Burlesque Show full of frilly frolics (try saying that ten times in a row!). Now the steamy windows have cleared, perhaps you would like to take advantage of your free drink refill? Because in 20 minutes we will be parting ways, until the next time...

Tempted? Book a table and enjoy a visual feast. Choose a Saturday and pick a slot: 1:30pm or 3:00pm. To secure your booking we recommend you book no later than one week in advance. The performances are not strictly unsuitable for children, but you must be prepared for a high number of bumps, grinds, and shimmies! Please get in touch to discuss our show content if you have any concerns. When booking your table, you will also pre-order your cakes & bakes. Hot and Cold soft drinks are available on the day with free refills before and after the show. Request menu.

The table needs to be paid for in advance at £12 per person. Cakes, bakes and drinks are billed to your table at the end of the event.

Drop us an email at karen@bodybarre.co.uk to secure a booking, or message us directly via Facebook to book your table.

Naughty Nights

Booze & Burlesque

Spend an evening with the Bombshells and enjoy a quirky burlesque show like no other. Expect the unexpected! Enjoy the beautifully lit venue and the delights of table service. Thats right, just take a seat and bathe in the beautiful surroundings and stand by for some sublime entertainment. Have a fabulous night sipping the delights from our hot and cold soft drinks menu or BYOB. We invite you to experience our flirty, fun shows and add a touch of glamour to your Saturday nights. Expect bold and mesmerising dance displays like no other. Are you in the mood? We just love to entertain! Get ready for a wildly fun experience. We’ll pop to your table to keep your drinks flowing as you sit back, relax and be dazzled by tassel twirlers and circus glitterati. When the clock reaches 9:30pm, that’s all folks. Get back on your feet and hit the street, until next time...

Fancy an alternative night out? Book a table for a tantalising treat. Choose a Saturday and get booked in for 7:30pm. To secure your booking we recommend you book no later than one week in advance. Shows are suitable for guests aged 18 and over. When booking your table, you can request the hot and cold soft drinks menu, to view which drinks will be available on the night.

The table needs to be paid for in advance at £18 per person. If you wish to to bring your own booze please respect our house rules as you will be subject to a corkage fee: £3 per bottle of wine and £1 per beer. Drinks are billed to your table at the end of the event.

Drop us an email at karen@bodybarre.co.uk to secure a booking, or message us directly via Facebook to book your table.
