Kids Aerial Circus - ages 4 to 16

Kids Aerial Circus classes are a great way for kids to get fit, meet new people and learn an interesting range of skills. Our classes are well-structured, with a clear progression throughout each term, we teach the kids how to become budding circus stars in a safe and fun environment.

The weekly sessions target a number of different skills throughout the term. We use specialised Circus apparatus including Aerial Silks, Static Trapeze, Aerial Hoop and Aerial Hammock.

Fun circus apparatus which makes exercise more active and exciting. No prior experience is required to join the fun.

Lets play upside down!

Kids Pole Sport - ages 6 to 16

A gymnastic class which works on the vertical pole in static and spinning mode.

Safety based and technique driven class instruction covers everything from fitness to performance. We combine the core elements of basic gymnastic skills and aerial dance.

Alongside learning lots of impressive moves, poses and tricks, we also work towards our grading badges and build full routines to music to perform at our yearly December show.

Children are encouraged to strive for, and reach individual goals, paced to their own ability, all within a non-competitive environment.
